What is phishing?
Phishing is a type of cyber attack where criminals pretend to be trustworthy companies (banks, PayPal, Amazon, etc.) to trick you into giving away passwords, credit card details, or personal information. They usually send fake emails or create fake websites that look real. Always check URLs carefully before entering any sensitive information!
